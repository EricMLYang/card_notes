### **[The Art of AI Product Development](https://learning.oreilly.com/library/view/the-art-of/9781633437050/)**

- *Misaligned data*—Your team failed to connect user needs with the data needed for training and evaluating the model. This is a common fallacy; it’s imperative to learn to mediate between your data and your users.

- **Best practice** Data alignment means ensuring that the training data used for AI reflects real-world user needs and scenarios
