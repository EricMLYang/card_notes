These examples demonstrate a range of AI applications that can be integrated into large-screen whiteboard devices. They include:

- **Handwriting and Shape Recognition:** Converting handwritten notes and drawings into digital text and shapes for easier editing and sharing.expand_more

- **Object Recognition:** Identifying objects placed on the board for interactive presentations and demonstrations.

- **Collaboration Features:** Facilitating remote collaboration through screen sharing, video conferencing, and digital whiteboarding tools.

- **Voice Assistants:** Enabling voice commands for controlling the device and accessing information.

Additionally, many of these devices offer the flexibility to install third-party Android apps, opening up possibilities for further AI integration.

By examining these existing products, you can gain valuable insights into the types of AI features that are in demand and the hardware configurations that best support them. This will help you make informed decisions when developing your own AI-powered whiteboard application.
