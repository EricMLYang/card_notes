## What is Reverse Engineering

- Reverse engineering refers to the systematic disassembly of an item in order to explore how it works internally and gain important insights.

- Reverse engineering refers to the process of looking beyond the obvious external appearances to uncover the underlying structures. These structures not only reveal how an item was designed but, more importantly, also show how it can be reproduced.
