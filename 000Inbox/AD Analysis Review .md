## \[ Main Reference \]

- **YouTube-Specific Metrics for Measuring Attention & Engagement:**

   - **View Rate:** The percentage of people who watched your ad after being served the impression.

   - **View-Through Rate (VTR):** The percentage of viewers who watched your ad to completion (or to a certain point, like 30 seconds).

   - Re-engagement Rate:

   - **Average Watch Time:** How long, on average, people watched your ad.

   - **Brand Lift Studies:** YouTube offers brand lift studies to measure the impact of your ads on metrics like brand awareness, ad recall, and purchase intent.

   - **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** If you are using interactive elements like cards or end screens, CTR measures how often people click on them.
